JULY 21st

CERES DAY 2022

Join us for our first ever Ceres Day! This is the moment to change it all. The Earth is changing, and it's changing fast. We need to do something about it! Now is the time for us to preserve and protect our health and families. A green future is more important than you think.

We need to act fast. While there is still time to solve the climate crisis, it is running out. It's time to choose the Earth and restore nature for our children and our children's children.

For Ceres Day we will be having special t-shirt designs where 100% of proceeds go towards our Roadmap 2030. As well as a blackout hour to promote sustainable living. Please join us in our first ever Ceres Day!
